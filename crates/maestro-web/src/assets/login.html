Dashboard sign in

Same URL every time. localhost and 127.0.0.1 are different sites for cookies. If you sign in on one and open the dashboard on the other, you will look logged out. Pick one hostname and stick to it (and use that host in bookmarks).

Credentials: [web] dashboard_username and dashboard_password in config. Check GET /api/auth/status for dashboard_auth_enabled if login always fails.
